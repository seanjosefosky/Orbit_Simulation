What is this game about?
- You are a rocket ship that travels between celestial bodies by launching into their orbits

Game Loop:
- player begins game orbiting around a celestial body
- player must launch ship in direction
    - the direction traveled is dependent on which point in orbit the ship was launched
- physics between the ship and celestial bodies will determine the outcome of the launch
    - ship speed, gravitational pull(s), vector(s)
- Different types of interferences will prevent the success of the rocket ship
    - asteroids, aliens, solar rays
- Game control(s) is similar to that of Jump King (Press a single key to launch)
    - Variation will depend on the ship's environment and the point from orbit the launch happened
